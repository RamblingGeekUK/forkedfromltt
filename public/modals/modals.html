Feedback & Suggestions

We welcome your feedback, suggestions, and creator submissions!

Please open a GitHub issue to submit feedback, report bugs, or suggest new creators.
(A GitHub account is required. This helps us keep track of all requests and suggestions in one place.)

  • For new creator suggestions, please include their YouTube channel link and any relevant info.
  • For bugs or site issues, describe the problem and steps to reproduce.

Thank you for helping improve the project!
